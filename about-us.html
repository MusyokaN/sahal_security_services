About Salhal Security Services.

SAHAL SECURITY SERVICES is a premier Private Security Corporation founded by distinguished leaders in the field of security and intelligence in Somalia. Established with a commitment to excellence and integrity, our company specializes in VIP Protection Services, Compound and Facilities Security, and Logistics, catering to the unique needs of our local and international clients.

Our team comprises highly trained professionals with extensive experience in military, law enforcement, and intelligence operations. We employ state-of-the-art technology and innovative strategies to ensure the highest level of protection and security for our clients.
